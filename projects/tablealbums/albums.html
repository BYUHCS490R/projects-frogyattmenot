Some infos

The Living Tombstone is an electronic rock group and Youtube channel. This group is notable for their songs and music videos based on video games and pop culture media. For example, the Five Nights at Freddy's series, Overwatch and My Little Pony, as well as original music like My Ordinary Life. They are still active until this day.
