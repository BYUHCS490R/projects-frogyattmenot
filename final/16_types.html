The 16 Personality Types

Four preference pairs combine to create 16 distinct personality types. Each individual has a preference from each pair. This forms a unique four-letter code. Below are the 16 personality types.

The 16 MBTI Personality Types
Personality Type Description
ISTJ (The Inspector) Responsible, organized, and practical, values tradition and loyalty.
ISFJ (The Protector) Caring, dependable, and detail-oriented, prioritizes the needs of others.
INFJ (The Advocate) Insightful, compassionate, and idealistic, seeks meaning and purpose.
INTJ (The Architect) Strategic, independent, and analytical, future-focused planners.
ISTP (The Virtuoso) Adaptable, practical, and action-oriented, enjoys hands-on activities.
ISFP (The Adventurer) Creative, sensitive, and spontaneous, appreciates beauty and new experiences.
INFP (The Mediator) Idealistic, empathetic, and introspective, values authenticity/novelty and harmony.
INTP (The Logician) Curious, logical, and innovative, enjoys exploring abstract concepts.
ESTP (The Entrepreneur) Energetic, bold, and perceptive, thrives in dynamic environments.
ESFP (The Entertainer) Outgoing, playful, and enthusiastic, lovess socializing and fun.
ENFP (The Campaigner) Imaginative, passionate, and sociable, inspire/motivates others with their enthusiasm.
ENTP (The Debater) Clever, curious, and outspoken, enjoys intellectual challenges.
