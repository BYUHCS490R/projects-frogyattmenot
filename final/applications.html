Applications of MBTI and Cognitive Functions

There are many ways MBTI and cognitive functions could be applied practically into your daily lives. With these knowleges, you will be able to be more self-aware of yourself and understand your own cognitive processes. Here are some real-world applications of MBTI and cognitive functions. To learn more, you can click here.

1. Finding love life

Having a good knowledge of MBTI types can be beneficial when it comes to searching a compatible companion. Knowing cognitive functions can further tell you the communication method with a partner and the best way to intake information from them.

2. Getting to know yourself better

While cognitive functions is on a broad scale, it can still offer some insights on a person's general habits. Knowing your dominant, auxiliary, tertiary, and inferior cognitive functions can make you more self-aware and empathetic toward others.

3. Make wiser career decisions

Understanding cognitive functions can inform how you process information. This helps determine the teams and work environments that best suit your needs.

4. Assists in problem solving

Cognitive functions is a good tool that tells the way different types of people navigate troubles, whether big or small. Understanding your own cognitive functions can aid you in problem solving depending on what suits you most.

5. Knowing your communication preferences

The MBTI cognitive functions can reveal a person's preferences for processing and speaking. For example, some people are more impulsive in sharing ideas while others prefer to sit with a prompt or question before responding.
