1. What is normal flow in HTML?

It refers to the default or original way that the browswr lays out the elements on a webpage before any special positioning or float rules are applied.

2. What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

The float property in css allows an element to move from left to right, letting theother content (text or images) to wrap around it. Floats can cause some layout issues because when you float an element, it is taken out of the normal flow of the document.
