Structural Tags

1. What goes in the header?

Header usually represents the introductory content or a set of navigational links.

The header usually contains:

  • one or more heading elements
  • logo or icon
  • authorship information

2. What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

What goes in the Nav are the major navigational links. The nav tag is used in the header tag. The nav tag does not have to be in the header tag. The nav tag does contain links to another website.

3. What goes in Main?

What goes inside the main is the dominant content of the body tag.

4. What goes in the Footer?

What goes in the footer are usually supplementary information such as copyright data or links to related documents.
