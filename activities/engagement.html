3. What are special entity characters?

Characters that are used to display characters that has a special meaning in HTML or that are not easily typed on a standard keyboard. All start with & and ends with ;. For example, 5 + 6 < 30 is wrong but 5+6 & lt ; 30 is correct, which gives 5+6 < 30.

4. What are phrase tags?

Tags that are used to provide structural and semantic meaning to parts of text in a HTML document, instead of just applying visual formatting.

5. What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

OL is an ordered list while UL is an unordered list.

6. What is a DL?

DL is descriptive list.
